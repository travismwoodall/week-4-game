Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. Each crystal has a hidden value, when clicked, it will be added to your total.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly selected number, you lose the game if your total goes above this number.

Each time the game starts the values of the crystals will change.
